Solving Tontine Challenges with Technology

My Smart ekimina was born from the real struggles faced by tontine leaders and members. We understand the pain points of manual reporting, unnecessary punishments, and complex loan processes. Our platform is designed specifically to address these challenges and make tontine management simple, transparent, and efficient for everyone.

Our Mission

To eliminate the administrative burdens of traditional tontines through digital automation, making group savings accessible, transparent, and free from unnecessary conflicts and punishments.

Our Vision

A world where every community savings group operates with digital efficiency, where contributions are automated, loans are accessible, and financial growth is accelerated through technology.

The Challenges We're Solving

Manual Report Preparation

Tontine leaders spend hours, sometimes days, preparing handwritten reports. Errors in calculations lead to disputes, mistrust, and unnecessary punishments for members who are actually not at fault.

Physical Meeting Struggles

Mandatory physical meetings exclude members who travel, are sick, or have family obligations. Missing meetings often results in unfair fines and punishments, creating financial strain and group conflicts.

Complex Loan & Payment Processes

Requesting loans requires multiple approvals and physical presence. Contribution payments are difficult to track, leading to confusion about who has paid and who hasn't, resulting in incorrect penalties.

The Result?

Tontine members face unnecessary stress and financial penalties. Leaders are overwhelmed with administrative work. Trust erodes, groups disband, and communities lose valuable financial support systems.
